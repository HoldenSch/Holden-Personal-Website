Welcome! My name is Holden Schermer and I was born and raised in beautiful Honolulu, Hawaii. Currently, I'm a sophomore at Harvard concentrating in Computer Science and Economics. I'm super passionate about using technology to help people and exploring the intersection of technology and finance. Because of this, in the future, I see myself as either a software engineer or product manager. When I'm not coding, you can find me trying out new foods, hitting the beach, hiking, doing CrossFit, or taking photos.
