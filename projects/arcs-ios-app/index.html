Arcs iOS App GitHub

Tech Stack: Swift, SwiftUI, Firebase, Cloud Functions, JavaScript

Role: Solo Developer

Arcs is a social accountability app that helps you stick to your habits by connecting you with friends. Instead of trying to build habits alone, you can create challenges with friends and track progress together. The app supports different types of challenges depending on what works best for your goals - photo challenges where you submit daily pictures, alarm challenges to help each other wake up on time, or simple notification check-ins for things like drinking water or taking breaks. Friends can invite each other to challenges and see how everyone is doing.

The app makes it easy to stay connected and motivated through a simple friend system using shareable codes. You can set up custom reminders, view activity from your challenge groups, and track your progress over time. Whether you're trying to exercise more consistently, wake up earlier, or just want to be more mindful about daily habits, Arcs provides a straightforward way to do it with friends rather than going it alone. The social element helps keep you accountable while making habit-building feel more like a shared activity than a chore.
